How I Confronted and Transformed my Inner Personalities has now been re-released. It introduces a new way to live, which is based on the understanding of the conscious and unconscious mind. The biochemical determinants of experience, emphasizing the role of dopamine, is described. Awareness and meditation techniques are used in the course of psychotherapy. The role of the underlying personalities in the unconscious mind is explored.
