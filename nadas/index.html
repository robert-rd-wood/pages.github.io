Welcome to johnnadas.com!

John Nadas, M.D.

This is the web site dedicated to the psychological ideas and techniques of John Nadas, M.D. His book The Cure for Everyday Hypnosis presents new theories of how the mind operates, and effective strategies to gain self-actualization. How I Confronted and Transformed my Inner Personalities - an autobiographical textbook of psychotherapy has now been re-released. The Book of Reality - The Neuroscience of the Teachings of Jesus places his theories into a new context. His psychological principles have been developed in his previous books, Transformation, Journey toward Energy, and Sweet Nonexistence. The essential elements of his teachings include energy, the unconscious mind, our inner personalities, dopamine, and our alter ego. Please browse through the pages of this web site to gather more information about his new psychology.
